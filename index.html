Jurassic Hunt

Catch. Clean. Cook

Jurassic Hunt is the experience of a lifetime. It is the chance to challenge yourself beyond your limit, either in the kitchen or on the hunting grounds. Our resort is located on Raptor Island, surrounded by pristine waters and secluded beaches it is the perfect place to take a breather. During your stay in Pangea this will be your home, all activities and tours depart and end at this location.


Here at Jurassic Hunt, we make your safety our number one priority. The perimeter of the Island has been outfitted with high-tech security and is constantly monitored. Our staff all have a military background and have been trained to handle any situation.
