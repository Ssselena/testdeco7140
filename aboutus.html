About Us

Jurassic Hunt is Pangea's only licensed safari hunting tour provider. Established in 2050 we now possess over 30 years experience. For the last 10 years, we are proud to offer a cooking course for our gourmet enthusiastic visitors, delighting them with exotic ingredients and aw-inspiring recipes.


Our resort is located on Raptor Island, a few miles from the coast of Pangea. For the convenience of our guests, we have now implemented time-travel stations in all major capitals of the world. Once the paperwork is done you will head to the nearest one where our experienced staff will guide you and your companions through the “Time-Jump”.
